Kinda Fancy Frito Pie

Description

Frito Pie is a popular junk food that combines chili, cheese, and corn chips. This version is a little fancier than what you'd get in a gas station, but not by much.

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs 80/20 ground beef
  • 1 white or yellow onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1-2 jalapenos, diced. Remove stems and seeds to reduce heat
  • 1 x 14.5 oz cans Diced tomatoes w/ chiles
  • 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 x 15 oz can Enchilada sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • Frito corn chips
  • Seasonings to taste, such as chili pepper, cayenne, parika
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Optional:1-2 cans Pinto or Kidney beans, drained

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°.
  2. Begin browning beef over medium heat in a greased, 12" cast iron skillet.
  3. Stir in the onion and caramelize. Add the peppers.
  4. Cook about 10 minutes, until beef is browned, onions are caramelized, and peppers are softened. Drain excess grease.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add in tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, Enchilada sauce, and seasonings. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat. Sprinkle evenly with cheese and place in pre-heated oven. Bake for 5-10 minutes, until cheese is melted.
  7. Top with Fritos and serve.
